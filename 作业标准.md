Individual Report [30%]
In this assessment, you will critically evaluate the product or service portfolio of an organisation and recommend a forward-thinking strategic direction. By applying two classic strategy tools [the BCG Matrix and the Ansoff Matrix], you will analyse the organisation's current position and develop evidence-based growth recommendations. 
You will select an organisation from the lecturer-approved list. You must use credible sources (peer-reviewed strategy articles, annual reports, market reports, reputable news databases, company websites, etc.).

General guidance to students on content and approach 
The word limit for this report is 2,000 words (plus or minus 10%), excluding the title page, table of contents, and references. You should indicate the word count on the cover of your assignment. 
References: APA 7th edition should be used. You must provide a minimum of 10 academic references. All sources must be cited in-text. 
Figures & Tables: These are optional. Figures and tables should be clearly labelled and cross-referenced and must be placed in the appendix (4 pages max). 
NOTE: The ability to communicate a cogent and coherent argument in a limited number of words is an academic skill required of students at level 3. Therefore, anything you write beyond 2,200 words will not be read. 
Formative assessment: This module allows you to seek formative feedback in class. 
Please note you will NOT be given an indicative grade. You will, however, be given hints and tips on improving your work. 
Assessment Criteria: The assessment criteria are below.
